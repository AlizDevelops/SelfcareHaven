About Us

We are five teenagers — Ali, Omar, Yassin, Mariam, and Malak — who share the same interests in programming, design, and expanding our knowledge about mental health and self-care.
We decided to share our knowledge by creating a simple website that offers effective solutions for various mental health and self-care challenges.
Our goal is to raise awareness about mental self-care, boost self-confidence for those who have experienced trauma, and help them take the first step towards a healthier and more fulfilling life.
