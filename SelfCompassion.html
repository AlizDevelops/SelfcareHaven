Self Compassion

Its easy to be kind to others, but often we forget to be kind to ourselves.
If you are struggling with self compassion, remember that you deserve the same care and understanding you offer to those around you.
It's okay to make mistakes and to have moments of doubt.
They don't define your worth. Be gentle to yourself during difficult times and try to treat yourself with the same warmth and empathy others get from you.
Self compassion is a journey on every small step you take towards being kinder to your self, which is a step towards healing.
Below are a few practices you can make to increase your self compassion




● Dedicate few moments to set quietly and focus on yourself.
- Although may sound basic, it does matter. Self reflect on your day and praise yourself for the positive impact you provided.

● Write about your feelings and changes
- Writing about your feelings is proven to work and make you get out any negativity, and when you write about your feelings you'll tend to think more clearly and be more organized.

● Create a list with good words to yourself and repeat them daily.

● May sound basic, but do what you love.
- Do something you enjoy and have maximum fun doing it.


"Self compassion is simply giving the same kindness to ourselves that we would give to others."

"Having compassion for yourself means that you honor and respect your humanness"
